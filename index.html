Our Story

About Primos

Primos Premier Pro Wrestling is Colorado's longest running independent wrestling promotion — a stomping ground for the wrestling superstars of yesterday, today, and tomorrow.

For over 14 years we've served the Rocky Mountain region with world-class live events and a training program — The Butcher Shop — that develops the next generation of professional wrestlers.

Proud LGBTQIA+ supporters 🏳️‍🌈 and hosts of all-inclusive pro wrestling seminars for individuals with developmental disabilities.
